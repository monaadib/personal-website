Amsterdam

  • august 12, 2018
  • some travels
  • by mona

Amsterdam is hip, maybe too hip for me.


Some things I enjoyed:

  • Dinner by a canal with Cindy.
  • Biking through Vondelpark.
  • Dealing with my mango obsession at Albert Cuyp Market (i.e. I bought mangoes and more mangoes).
  • Resistance museum, especially the Risk of Explosion exhibit which describes an attack on the Amsterdam Registry Office organized by a group of artists.
  • Visiting different churches in Utrecht. Each type of church (Catholic, Lutheran, Mennonite) has a completely different feel.

Something I noticed about myself is that when I see a fountain, I have to put my feet in it. Also, mom, here is the picture you wanted of me and my bike.
