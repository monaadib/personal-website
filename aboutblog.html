Blog Happenings

I never planned for the blog to be any good and I don't think it will ever be good (oh boy, there's the missing confidence again). My mom told me that I should turn this into a travel blog -- you know, the kind with tips and tricks and posts titled "BEST 10 THINGS TO DO IN PARIS". I think we both know that I lack the planning and maturity for something like that. Instead, I see this blog as being a jumble of my thoughts or something like that. ¯\_(ツ)_/¯

To be frank, I think the lack of context and fluidity in my blog posts is a pretty good reflection of the way I think. But there are definitely some things that I would like to change and do with the blog. Here are some things to expect in the future (I'm prone to procrastination so future could mean many different things):


  • A vlog of Christmas time at my house. I love my family, Michael Buble and the ho-ho-holiday spirit and I want to capture just that.
  • A record of most of the things that made me laugh during an entire day. I have to figure out how I would even keep track of this because I laugh every 10 minutes.
  • Discussions about how my favourite books make me feel.
  • Taking and posting selfies. Yea except for sending weird faces to friends on Snapchat or being forced by friends to take a group selfie, I never take solo selfies. Taking pictures of myself makes me vERy UncOmFOrtAbLe.
  • A video compilation of my cat Cyrus' cutest moments.
  • More hip, aesthetic, spunky visuals on the blog.
  • Etc.

I'm still trying to figure out what I want this blog to be. I'm a mess and the blog is currently a mess, but I think I like the messiness. So expect a lot of that.
