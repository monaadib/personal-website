Mona Adib

Welcome to the blog, and here's a little about me.

Version you probably know:

Hi there! I'm Mona and I'm an incoming third year student at the University of British Columbia. I'm loud. I make myself laugh and I like to think I make others laugh. Sometimes I smile so much that my cheeks feel strained, and every day at some point my stomach hurts from laughing too hard -- usually at my own joke. I'm indecisive about what to eat unless Persian is an option. I love puns and books that make me laugh, cry and then laugh again. I can't watch a scary movie without staying up for days. Every year, my New Year's resolution is to stop cussing, and every January 2nd I rethink my decisions. I feel my happiest when I'm with my mom, sister and cat and I feel my most calm and comfortable when I'm near water.

Version I would like you to know:

Hi there! I'm Mona and I'm not very confident. I often doubt myself and compare myself to others. I stress about meeting new people and sometimes stress about seeing old friends. I care so much about what people think of me that I spend nights pacing around my room remembering everything I said and did during the day. I dread being in front of a camera. I'm scared to tell people that I don't agree with them. I'm also scared to tell people that I want to be alone.


I'm not confident, but I'm trying to be. So I made a blog.
